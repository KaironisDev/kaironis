TCT Trading Dictionary:
● POI = Point of Interest, area's in the chart where we will look for TCT entries
● HP = High Probability
● HPS = High Probability Setup
● MS = Market Structure
● TP = Take Profit
● SL = Stoploss
● SFP = Swing Failure Pattern = wick
● RTZ = Return To Zone = liquidity curve
● OB = Orderblock
● FVG = Fair Value Gap
● PO3 = Power of 3
● MO = Market Order
● LO = Limit Order
● HTF = High Timeframe
● MTF = Mid Timeframe
● LTF = Low Timeframe
● SOW = Signs Of Weakness
● SOS = Signs Of Strenght
● RR = Risk to Reward
● VAH = Value Area High (upper horizontal line on your volume profile)
● VAL = Value Area Low (lower horizontal line on your volume profile)
● POC = Point of Control (red horizontal line on your volume profile)
● SS = Structure Supply
● SD = Structure Demand
● MM = Market Maker
● $$$ = liquidity
● DL = Deviation Limit
● CMP = Current Market Price
● STTP = Stick to the Plan
● OBIF = Orderblock with Ineffiency (valid OB is always OBIF)
● EL = Extreme Liquidity
● ES = Extreme Supply
● ED = Extreme Demand
● TT = Technical Target
● EHP = Extra High Probability
