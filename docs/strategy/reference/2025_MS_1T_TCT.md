There might have been some confusion around the concept because the
importance of the structure pool is not connected to the timeframe that we're
pulling it on, but rather the way that we are pulling the structure. So either from
bottom to top, or top to bottom and therefore the terminology level 1, 2 and 3.
It showcases the different characteristics of each individual market structure
pool, rather than a timeframe.
